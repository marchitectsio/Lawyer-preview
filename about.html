How we work

A structured process, not chaos

Every case is different, but the system follows a pattern. We use that pattern to protect you early and build leverage for dismissal, reduction, or the best trial posture available.

Step 1

The arrest

Stay calm and stay silent. Do not try to explain the situation to police. Ask for a lawyer. Document names, locations, and any witnesses the moment you can do so safely.

Step 2

The arraignment

This is the first court appearance. Bail and protective orders are often decided here. We appear with you, ask for reasonable release conditions, and set the case up for strong defense work.

Step 3

Pre trial hearings

We challenge the evidence. That includes suppression motions, credibility issues, and technical flaws in testing or police procedure. Many cases are won here long before a trial date.
